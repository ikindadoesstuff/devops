# USE CASE: 6 View Employee's Details

## CHARACTERISTIC INFORMATION

### Goal in Context

As an *HR advisor* I want to *view a new employee's details* so that *the employee's promotion request can be supported.*

### Scope

Employee Management System.

### Level

Primary task.

### Preconditions

We know the employee's name. Employee exists in the database.

### Success End Condition

Employee details are available to HR Advisor.

### Failed End Condition

No employee details are retrieved.

### Primary Actor

HR Advisor.

### Trigger

Employee sends a promotion request to HR.

## MAIN SUCCESS SCENARIO

1. HR advisor selects option to add employee into the system.
2. HR advisor enters employee information.
3. HR advisor reviews info.
4. HR advisor confirms employee addition.

## EXTENSIONS

3. **Entered Information is Incorrect**:
   1. HR advisor revises entered information.
   2. HR advisor adds employee.

## SUB-VARIATIONS

None.
